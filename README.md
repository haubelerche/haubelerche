## About Me

I am an aspiring **AI Conversation Engineer** with hands-on experience in **LLM-powered systems, Vietnamese NLP pipelines, RAG, GraphRAG, multi-agent workflows, and speech-focused AI applications**.

My current focus is building AI systems that can understand user intent, retrieve grounded knowledge, respond naturally, and operate reliably in real-world conversational environments. I am especially interested in the intersection of **Conversational AI, Speech Processing, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and AI System Evaluation**.

I aim to become an engineer who does not only build models, but also designs complete AI products with strong attention to **accuracy, latency, safety, observability, and user experience**.
